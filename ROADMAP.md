## Project Roadmap

> *"A roadmap is more than a timeline—it is a commitment to deliver value through disciplined execution."*

---

# Project Vision

The Enterprise Platform Engineering Handbook aims to become a comprehensive, vendor-neutral reference for designing, building, operating, and evolving enterprise software platforms.

The handbook will combine architectural principles, engineering best practices, implementation guidance, governance models, and real-world examples into a single cohesive reference.

Unlike technology-specific books, this handbook focuses on **why architectural decisions are made before explaining how they are implemented**.

---

# Mission

Our mission is to produce a publication-quality handbook that enables engineers and architects to:

* Understand enterprise architecture evolution.
* Design modern platform engineering capabilities.
* Build scalable cloud-native platforms.
* Standardize engineering practices.
* Improve software delivery.
* Make informed architectural decisions.

---

# Guiding Principles

The project is guided by the following principles.

* Architecture before Technology.
* Explain Why before How.
* Enterprise-first Design.
* Vendor-neutral Architecture.
* AWS as the primary implementation reference.
* Practical examples over theoretical discussions.
* Continuous review and improvement.
* Incremental delivery.
* Engineering discipline throughout the authoring process.
